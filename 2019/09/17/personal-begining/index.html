Who am I

long story short

A normal CS guy who want to share what he has learned, helping himself, even others in programming.

long story long

I’m just a normal CS person. I’m not smart, or hard-working like others. I’m really emontional that could easily get frustrated.
I slept to noon, got drunk, dragged on everything, I even took final exams while I am drunk. Suspected about myselft, about my life.
Because I got frustrated due to some stupid reasons. I didn’t have the courage to face it, so I decided to hide from it.

What a coward.

But I realized that I can’t be that anymore. I starting to face it, step by step. Finally, I ended my university life with 3.9 (3 As and 1 A-, sad) in the last semester, 3/4 were CS courses. THIS MADE ME REALLY HAPPY, I seed my rewards. I’m returning.

I applied Huawei in my spare time when I back to China due to some personal business. I passed the online assessment, I got the interview chance, I passed the first tech interview, but failed before the final interview. I got no frustrated feeling. OK, to be honest, there was a little bit. But I realized that I really came through that stupid period.

Life is a marathon, I just got some obstacles, it’s not even half so don’t give up, not only me, but also you.

Zheng Liang
2019.9.17, late night
